# Project Context

This website represents the furry persona Sidney.

The goal is to introduce the character and provide
links to community platforms.

---

# Tone

Friendly  
Welcoming  
Community-oriented

Avoid corporate or marketing language.

---

# Content Focus

The site focuses on:

- fursona identity
- events attended
- social media presence

---

# Target Audience

Visitors from the furry fandom.

Typical visitors include:

- convention attendees
- online community members
- friends from the fandom

---

# Visual Style

The site should feel:

friendly  
modern  
clean

Green is the primary color.

---

# UX Goals

Visitors should quickly understand:

- who Sidney is
- where Sidney can be found online
- which events Sidney attended

---

End of context.
